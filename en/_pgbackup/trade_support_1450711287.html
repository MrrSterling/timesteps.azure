Trading support

TS-Energy has a variety of applications in the area of energy trading. The software provides you with pricing and risk analysis for a broad spectrum of energy contracts. Also, for virtual power plants and ancillary services optimal prices are calculated.

Front Office
The stochastic dynamic programming of TS-Energy generates hourly margin prices for the intra-day and day-ahead trading. These calculations happen based on input data such as fill levels, inflow projections and possible restrictions (for pumped-storage power plants): The margin prices serve as minimum and maximum prices at which electricity should be traded. It has been shown that this approach leads to significantly better results that other bidding generation methods.

TS-Energy as part of the day-ahead trading. It generates price-dependent offers and optimizes the production of the agreed upon amount of energy.

Middle Office
The participation int the ancillary services market can be a profitable alternative to marketing your flexibilities. TS-Energy allows you the comprehensive mapping of the ancillary services and a precise determination of the opportunity costs. Because of that TS-Energy enables an improved bidding generation and significant profit increases.   

Back Office
TS-Energy also generates and analyzes valuations and risk analyzes of your energy portfolios. Additionally TS-Energy is capable of providing the optimal allocation of any set of hedging instruments based on a selectable criterion such as securing production, securing value or minimizing value at risk. The calculations can be provided on a daily basis, even for larger power plant complexes, in order to provide the necessary decision-making basis for the trading.
